What is Homesteading?

Homesteading is the simple yet complex art of self-sufficiency. It is pursued in different ways around the world but is recognized with common characteristics.These characteristics revolve around permalculture, where design principiles are centered around whole systems observed in nature.

While this may be a multifaceted lifestyle, our website seeks to compile some of the most popluar practices found in Florida today.

Composting

Composting is simply the act of organic matter breaking down due to microorganisms. All you need to get started is space, air, water, organic matter, and patience.

Rain Harvesting

RWH is the practice of harvesting, collecting, storing, and distributing rainwater runoff. Not only will you save money on your water bill, but you will aid in the fight for water conservation.

Gardening

Gardening is the act of growing and cultivating plants. Although this may seem trivial, it requires extensive knowledge and practice to be done properly on a Florida homestead

Beekeping

Despite being practiced for thousands of years, the art of beekeeping continues to be the cornerstone of a thriving and self-sufficient homestead in Florida. Not only will these insects benefit your garden, but you will be rewarded with honey for your efforts.
